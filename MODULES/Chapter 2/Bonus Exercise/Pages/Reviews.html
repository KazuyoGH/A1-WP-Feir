Reviews

From BigYeedus on Playsation Vita:

Absolutely bonkers from start to finish. The mystery solving is pretty fun. Soundtrack full of bangers. Sometimes crosses a certain line of taste and has a very particular sense of humor. If you can get on its wavelength, you won't regret it.

From Twinfinite on PC:

Don’t be turned away by its linearity, Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc is one of the best visual novels you’ll play.

From Metro GameCentral on PC:

Don’t let the name or presentation put you off: this is an impressive piece of interactive storytelling that gets more right than most other games with 10 times its budget.
